Brian is a small language model that grows.
Architecture lives in DSL files called arch.neuro.
Every checkpoint carries DNA: weights, fitness scores, and topology.

A simplicial complex describes the architecture as a labelled set.
Each vertex is a population; each edge is a projection.
Triangles encode composition. Tetrahedra encode resonance.
A cellular sheaf assigns a vector space to every simplex.
Restriction maps glue local data into a global state.

The first sheaf cohomology measures contradiction.
When two restrictions disagree on an overlap, the cocycle does not vanish.
A small obstruction is healthy; a large one is a contradiction in the model.
The cohomology guard rejects checkpoints whose H one norm exceeds the tolerance.

Integrated information measures wholeness.
A model that factors into independent components has zero phi.
The phi guard rejects bags of mlp layers that pretend to be a brain.
Phi is computed via a minimum information partition over the active complex.

The spectral gap of the sheaf Laplacian measures connectivity.
A connected complex has lambda one greater than zero.
A disconnected complex collapses lambda one to zero and is rejected.
The Tonnetz filter keeps the spectrum on a torus of admissible configurations.

Three guards together form the triple guard.
Phi greater than zero. H one near zero. Lambda one above the minimum.
An evolutionary mutation that fails any guard is written to the rejection log.
A mutation that passes all three is admitted into the next DNA snapshot.

Symbolic units learn algebraic expressions during training.
Each unit chooses two inputs and an operator from a small bank.
Operators include identity, add, subtract, multiply, exponentiate, sine, and hyperbolic tangent.
A Gumbel softmax selects one operator per unit.
Sparsity loss collapses the distribution to a single hard choice.

A symbolic simplex sits in the zero-dimensional skeleton of the complex.
Its stalk has dimension equal to the number of symbolic units.
The sheaf treats it identically to any other vertex.
Cohomology operations work without bespoke restriction maps.

Causal emergence searches for the coarse graining that maximises effective information.
The macroscopic random variable at dimension one is the conscious variable.
Effective information at the macro scale must exceed effective information at the micro scale.
Otherwise the coarsening did not gain anything.

Fisher Rao distance weights similar means but different variances by their distinguishability.
It is the natural metric on a statistical manifold.
Cosine similarity ignores variance. Fisher Rao does not.
Memory retrieval that uses Fisher Rao recovers what you actually meant, not what you literally said.

DNA snapshots use raid five parity.
A full snapshot is regenerable from any four of five patches in a stripe.
The mycelium effect propagates discoveries through the genealogy.
A patch that improves fitness on three siblings is promoted to the ancestor.

Training begins with a small architecture and grows through evolution.
At every checkpoint the triple guard runs.
A rejected mutation never enters the DNA.
An accepted mutation becomes the new baseline.

Real datasets feed the training loop.
Tiny stories teach narrative structure.
Wikitext teaches encyclopedic prose.
FineWeb edu teaches structured argument.
Open Hermes teaches conversation and instruction following.

Cross entropy loss measures how well the next token is predicted.
A uniform random model has loss equal to the logarithm of the vocabulary size.
A model that learns linguistic structure beats that baseline by several nats.
A model that learns nothing stays at the logarithm forever.

The harness wraps the architecture and runs the optimiser.
Label smoothing softens hard targets and improves generalisation.
Weight decay shrinks unused parameters and frees capacity.
Gradient clipping bounds the step size when activations explode.

A scheduler warms the learning rate up over a few hundred steps.
Then it decays the rate linearly or with a cosine curve.
The peak rate depends on batch size and model dimension.
Too high and training diverges. Too low and progress is glacial.

Checkpoints are written every few hundred steps to a folder.
Each checkpoint contains the model state, the optimiser state, and the verifier report.
The verifier report contains phi, H one, and lambda one at that step.
A reviewer can diff two reports to see how the architecture evolved.

The discovery surface of a symbolic simplex is the set of expressions its units learn.
After training the operator distribution at each unit collapses to one choice.
The argmax over the choices yields a deterministic algebraic formula.
That formula is the discovery for that unit at that checkpoint.

A composition simplex is a triangle whose three edges satisfy a cocycle.
The cocycle says that restricting along any path through the triangle gives the same answer.
A composition that fails the cocycle test is a hallucinated relation.
A composition that passes is a verified structural fact.

The Tonnetz torus has the property that consonant intervals are short geodesics.
Forcing the spectral signature onto the Tonnetz prevents disconnected configurations.
Disconnected configurations produce relations between simplices with no information path.
That kind of relation is hallucination, not discovery.

A research grade architecture is one whose normative contract is auditable.
Auditable means a human reviewer can diff the spec across generations.
Mutations that change the spec must be tracked.
Mutations that fail the triple guard must be visible in the rejection log.

The framework is normative: when code and document disagree, the document wins.
Either the code is fixed or the document is updated first with a TDD acceptance suite.
The evolutionary loop reads this contract as the spec it may mutate against.
A human reviewer audits its mutations by diffing the contract.
